Our Story

Discover the journey behind Pankhuri's Cakes & Kitchen

From Humble Beginnings to Sweet Success

It all started in a small kitchen in Navi Mumbai. Pankhuri’s passion for baking grew from making cakes for family celebrations to crafting delicious, customized cakes and desserts for people all over the city. Our mission has always been to create healthy, eggless treats with love and care.

Pankhuri’s Cakes & Kitchen is built on the belief that great desserts bring people together. Over the years, we’ve grown, but the heart and soul of our business remains the same – to spread happiness through the joy of baking.

Whether it’s a birthday, anniversary, or just a regular day, we take pride in making your celebrations sweeter with our freshly baked goodies.
