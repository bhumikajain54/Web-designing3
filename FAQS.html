Frequently Asked Questions

Below are answers to some of the most commonly asked questions about our bakery and services.

What types of cakes do you offer?

We offer a wide variety of cakes including chocolate, vanilla, red velvet, and more. You can also order customized cakes for special occasions.

Do you make eggless cakes?

Yes, all our cakes are eggless and made with the finest ingredients.

How far in advance should I place my order?

We recommend placing your order at least 2-3 days in advance, especially for custom cakes. For standard cakes, we can often accommodate same-day orders.

Do you offer delivery?

Yes, we offer delivery across Navi Mumbai and nearby areas. You can also pick up your order from our bakery.

Can I customize my cake?

Absolutely! We specialize in custom cakes. You can provide your design ideas, flavors, and specifications, and we will create a cake tailored to your needs.
